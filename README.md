🐑 Qurban Kəsdir - Official Landing Page
Sadə. Şəffaf. Güvənli. Şəriətə uyğun.[cite: 1]
This is the official promotional website for the Qurban Kəsdir mobile application[cite: 1]. The platform is designed to digitize and bring full transparency to the Qurban tradition in Baku, Azerbaijan[cite: 1].

🎯 Our Mission
The Qurban market in Baku sees 60,000-70,000 animals slaughtered annually[cite: 1]. However, many people face three core challenges:

Finding a Reliable Butcher: Is it Sharia-compliant? Is the butcher certified?[cite: 1]

Unclear Pricing: Hidden costs and surprise fees at the last minute[cite: 1].

Lack of Proof: No physical evidence that the Qurban was performed in your name correctly[cite: 1].

Qurban Kəsdir solves these by providing a digital-first approach with video proof and fixed pricing[cite: 1].

✨ Key Features
🌍 Bilingual Experience (AZ/EN)
Content is dynamically served based on a user-controlled language slider[cite: 1].

All strings are stored in an H2 Database, allowing for easy updates without code changes[cite: 1].

📹 Total Transparency
Adın Çəkilir: The butcher calls the owner's name out loud during the process[cite: 1].

Video Sübut: A full video of the slaughter and packaging is sent directly to the user's WhatsApp[cite: 1].

🤝 Social Impact (Charity Integration)
Users can choose to deliver the meat to Orphanages (Uşaqlar Evi) or Nursing Homes (Qocalar Evi)[cite: 1].

Dual Video Proof: Donors receive two videos-one of the slaughter and one of the delivery/handover[cite: 1].
