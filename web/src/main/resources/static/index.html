Three Major Problems with Sacrificing in Baku

1

Finding Reliable Butchers is Difficult

Trusting someone with your sacrifice is difficult. Who is the butcher? What is their quality? Are you satisfied with the result?

2

No Price Certainty

First they quote one price, then it changes. Hidden costs appear. Wasted money...

3

No Transparency or Proof

How was the animal slaughtered? Where is the meat? Is the animal dead? Do you know? You have no video. No proof.
